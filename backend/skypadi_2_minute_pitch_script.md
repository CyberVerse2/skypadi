# Skypadi 2-Minute Pitch Script

Hi, I’m building **Skypadi**: a WhatsApp-first flight booking assistant for African travellers.

The problem is simple. Booking a flight still takes too much work. When I want the best flight, I often have to check multiple airline and travel websites, repeat the same route and date search, compare prices manually, then fill another set of passenger and payment forms when I finally choose. It is slow, repetitive, and annoying.

Skypadi fixes that by letting travellers say the trip once inside WhatsApp.

For example, I can type: “Find me the cheapest Lagos to Abuja flight tomorrow afternoon.” Skypadi searches across options, compares fares, timing, baggage, and restrictions, then returns the best choices in plain language. Once I choose, Skypadi reuses my passenger details, sends a secure payment flow, and keeps my ticket, PNR, itinerary, and support in the same WhatsApp thread.

We’re starting with Nigeria, then expanding across African local, regional, and international flight corridors. This is a multi-billion-dollar booking market. IATA projects African carriers to generate about **$200 million in net profit in 2026 at a 1% margin**, which implies roughly **$20 billion in annual airline revenue** for African carriers alone. If Skypadi helps capture just **1% of that booking value**, that is about **$200 million in annual flight GMV** flowing through the platform.

In Nigeria alone, aviation revenue is projected to reach **$2.58 billion by 2029**, so 1% of that beachhead is about **$25.8 million in annual flight GMV**.

Skypadi monetizes that GMV through transparent convenience fees, supplier margins, ancillaries like baggage and transfers, and later SME travel desk subscriptions.

Our go-to-market is focused: start with a few high-frequency local routes, prove quote-to-book conversion, then expand into regional and international corridors.

The first proof is our WhatsApp demo. We want to show that travellers can state a trip once, compare options, and move toward booking without repeating the same forms across multiple sites.

Skypadi is not another travel app. It is the fastest way to compare and book flights from the channel African travellers already use every day: WhatsApp.
